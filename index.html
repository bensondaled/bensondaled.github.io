LLM-based AI for healthcare

AI researcher at a healthtech startup, engineering and evaluating new AI tools to enhance healthcare delivery.

Foundation model for intraoperative medicine

Building and validating foundation models to unlock the predictive power of intraoperative data and guide clinical decisions (manuscript in revision).

How AI agents become intelligent

Characterizing how the cognitive strategies of AI agents compare to those observed in neuroscience (at Google DeepMind), published and presented at NeurIPS.

Non-human and machine consciousness

Applying insights from neuroscience and clinical medicine to prepare for safe and ethical interactions with non-traditional consciousness.

How humans learn causal relationships

Bayesian modeling of human inference strategies for inferring causality between observed events.

Modeling and shaping memory and decisions

Quantifying how animals retain information in memory and use it to guide decisions, using measurement and perturbation of neural networks during learning and behavior.

Transcranial ultrasound neuromodulation

Noninvasively modulating brain circuits with high spatial precision to enhance the way medications influence the brain (study ongoing, ask me for details).

Neuroengineering with anesthetics

Developing software and hardware to perform closed-loop behavioral, pharmacologic, and EEG-based human studies.

Psychoactive medication trials

Running clinical studies to test therapeutic applications of psychedelics and anesthetic medications for new purposes (in peer review and trials ongoing).

Neurologic complications in ECMO and cardiac arrest

Identifying risk factors for neurologic injury in patients receiving cardiac mechanical support.

Ketamine neurophysiology

Demonstrating that distinct cortical signatures of ketamine dissociate under wakefulness and anesthesia.

Cerebellar calcium imaging

Studying neuronal encoding rules using cellular-resolution two-photon imaging of the rodent brain.

Teaching

Global health education
